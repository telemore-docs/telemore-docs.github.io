Getting Started

Welcome to Telemore AI! We are excited that you want to learn more about our application. Within these docs, you will find usage instructions. If you have any questions, comments, or feedback, please reach out to support@telemore.ai or submit a request on our online portal here.

Overview​

Instead of manually tracking tasks, writing documents, or juggling different tools, Telemore AI serves as your intelligent productivity co-pilot. It streamlines your workflows by integrating AI directly into your task and documentation processes. Whether you’re planning a project, drafting content, or managing day-to-day operations, Telemore AI helps you stay organized, automate repetitive work, and focus on what matters. It’s a unified platform designed to help you manage, execute, and scale your productivity with the support of smart, context-aware AI—fully embedded into your existing work environment.

Core Features​

Telemore AI offers a powerful set of core features designed to enhance how you think, plan, and create. Here’s what each core area unlocks:

✅ Tasks​

Track and manage your to-do items with intelligent assistance. Telemore understands the context of your work, helping you capture next steps, update progress, and stay organized effortlessly.

📄 Docs​

Create rich, structured documents—from strategies and research to outlines and storytelling. Powered by the same backend as Perplexity AI, Telemore helps you generate high-quality content with context from your existing work.

🎤 Talk​

Use speech-to-text transcription to capture ideas without typing. Just speak your thoughts and convert them into text with a simple shortcut. In private beta, you can also interact with a voice agent—an AI coach that helps you plan, reflect, and make decisions through natural conversation.

💬 Chat​

A built-in LLM-powered chatbot that does more than answer questions. It understands your tasks and documents, allowing it to brainstorm with you, write, edit, summarize, or help navigate any part of your workflow.

Installation​

To get started with Telemore AI:

  1. From a Mac or Windows computer, download the application from the home page. Note that installations are by operating system, linux support coming soon.
  2. Once downloaded, double-click on the downloaded file to install.
  3. Create an installed application and create an account. Note that you will have to verify your email before your account is created.
  4. Once verified, just sign in! As a welcoming gift, enjoy our 10-day free trial. After which, subscribe to unlock full features. More info on subscription plans can be found here.
