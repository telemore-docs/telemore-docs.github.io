Chat

Chat is a core part of the Telemore AI experience—designed to feel like a real collaborator, not just a tool. Whether you’re brainstorming, managing tasks, reviewing documents, or navigating complex decisions, the built-in chatbot is always available to help you move faster and think better.

You can have natural conversations with the chatbot, ask questions about your projects, and even upload files to get summaries, insights, or transcriptions. It’s context-aware, meaning it knows about your documents, your tasks, and what you’ve been working on—so your assistant is always aligned with your goals.

Features​

Use these helpful chat features:

  • Understands Attachments and Derives Insights
    Upload PDFs, spreadsheets, audio files, or notes—Telemore’s chatbot can analyze the content, extract key information, summarize sections, and even answer your questions about the material. It's like having a built-in research assistant.

  • Knows About Your Tasks & Docs
    The chatbot is context-aware. It understands your current documents and tasks, enabling it to offer relevant, personalized guidance without needing manual input or re-explanation.

  • Automates Creation and Updates Without the Manual Writing
    Skip the blank page. The chatbot can generate documents, update existing ones, and create or modify tasks automatically. You simply describe what you need—it handles the structure and wording.

  • Real-Time Search Capabilities for the Latest Information
    Need to verify a fact or find recent data? The chatbot can search the web in real time to bring you accurate, up-to-date results for fast, informed decisions.

  • Helps With Questions to Get Your Work Done
    Whether you're clarifying a strategy, debugging a plan, or figuring out next steps, the chatbot can guide you with answers, resources, or actionable recommendations.

  • Full Screen Mode
    Enter distraction-free mode for deep focus when chatting with your assistant. Ideal for immersive planning, writing, or reviewing large responses.

  • Speech-to-Text Transcription
    Speak your thoughts or upload audio recordings—your chatbot will convert them into clear, searchable text. Great for meetings, brainstorming, or capturing ideas on the go.

Chat Tools Available​

Tools are how LLM-based chatbots interact with your data. They allow the AI to take action—whether that’s managing your tasks, handling documents, or searching the web on your behalf.

📝 Task Management Tools​

Manage and stay on top of your to-dos with ease.

  • Create Task: Start a new task by providing a title, description, due date, and optional email reminder.
  • Update Task: Modify an existing task—change the description, update the status, or adjust the due date.

📄 Document Tools​

Easily find and manage your files using natural language.

  • Search Documents: Describe what you’re looking for, and the AI will find relevant documents.
  • Read Document: Retrieve a specific document using its ID.
  • Update Document: Edit the content of an existing document.

🌐 Web Search Tool​

Get real-time information from across the internet.

  • Search the Web: Ask a question or request information, and the AI will search the web for accurate, up-to-date answers.

These tools help you complete tasks faster—whether you’re organizing work, managing documents, or looking for the latest information. Just tell me what you need, and I’ll take it from there.

Important Notes​

  • Updating docs and tasks will only prepend text, and will not directly modify existing content. This safeguard exists as LLMs are probabilistic and may make mistakes when editing long documents. To make content changes to a full doc, we recommend copy and pasting the contents directly from Chat to ensure you are satisfied witht the changes.

  • Clear chat history when starting new topics. This reduces your usage limit and keeps the LLM focused on relevant information. Too much unrelated information degrades answer quality (an inherent limitation of LLMs).

  • Subscribing gives you significantly higher chat rate limits. If you reached your usage limits, you will receive a message in response.

Starting Conversations​

To start a new chat:

  1. Type your message and press enter
  2. Optionally use microphone transcription
  3. Attach files if desired

Chat Best Practices​

  • Be precise in your requests. Specific wording like "update task" or "return just information" helps control LLM behavior to get what you want
  • Reset the chat history often to help the LLM stay focused and reduce usage on account limits
  • Chat histories are not recorded after clearing. Save important information to docs
  • "Create docs" tool is only meant for research docs (powered by Perplexity). Other workflows like creating docs from chat history are not yet supported
